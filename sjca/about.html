About Us

Founded with a mission to empower individuals through faith and purpose, St. Jude Career Alliance blends spiritual guidance with practical resources to help people navigate their professional journeys. Since our inception, we’ve been committed to fostering careers that align with personal values, offering workshops, networking events, and mentorship rooted in our spiritual foundation. Our community-driven approach inspires growth, connection, and fulfillment.
